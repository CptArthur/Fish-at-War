# Description

> This mod is an extension for the dev version of the Ares at War scenario. It adds areas onto the map of the planet Agaris, where fish can be found. By using the net block contained within this mod, fish can be caught. Those fish can then be sold to the various settlements on the planet.

# How To Use

Build a Trawling Net block onto your ship and travel to a fish-rich area. These areas are indicated on the ingame map of Agaris added by the [AAW Navigation Map V2](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3535061555) mod. Once in one such area, deploy the net via terminal or hotbar action. Depending on your speed, the Efficiency of catching fish will go up or down. The Net Content percentage shows, how full the net is. This information can also be retrieved from the LCD App this mod adds.

At any point you can retrieve the net using the terminal, which will spawn your catch as fish items into the inventory of the Trawling Net Block.

# Features

* 1 Net block - the Trawling Net block.
* LCD App script to display net status.
